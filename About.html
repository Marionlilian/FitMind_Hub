About FitMind Hub

Where mental wellness meets compassion. We're dedicated to supporting your healing journey.

Our Story

FitMind Hub was born out of a shared vision to make mental health support accessible, personalized, and stigma-free for everyone in Kenya and beyond. Founded in 2025, we saw a growing need for a safe online space where people could find the right therapist, track their well-being, and connect with others facing similar struggles. Whether you're managing anxiety, grief, or burnout, we're here to walk the journey with you—at your pace, in your own way.

Meet Our Founder

Dr. Amina Wanjiru

Clinical Psychologist, Mental Health Advocate

Amina’s passion for mental wellness is deeply rooted in her experience working with underserved communities across Kenya. She envisioned FitMind Hub as a digital sanctuary—a space for care, connection, and empowerment. Her commitment to culturally sensitive care is reflected in everything we do.
